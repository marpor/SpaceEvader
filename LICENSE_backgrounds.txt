Map background image credits and terms

The background files listed below are processed/cropped game backgrounds derived
from third-party space and Earth imagery. They are not covered by
LICENSE_assets.txt and have their own terms as stated below:

General source policies checked

- NASA Images and Media Usage Guidelines:
  https://www.nasa.gov/nasa-brand-center/images-and-media/
  NASA content generally is not subject to copyright in the United States,
  but NASA must not be used to imply endorsement. NASA logos, identifiable
  persons, promotional uses, and third-party copyrighted material have extra
  restrictions.

- NASA Earth Observatory Image Use Policy:
  https://earthobservatory.nasa.gov/image-use-policy
  Most NASA Earth Observatory material may be reused, including for commercial
  purposes, unless copyright is indicated. NASA asks for credit.

- JPL Image Use Policy:
  https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/jpl-image-use-policy/
  Unless otherwise noted, images and videos on JPL public web sites may be
  used for any purpose without prior permission. Credit is required, and no
  NASA/JPL/Caltech endorsement may be claimed or implied.

- ESA/Hubble Copyright Information:
  https://esahubble.org/copyright/
  ESA/Hubble images, videos, and web texts are released under the Creative
  Commons Attribution 4.0 International license unless explicitly stated
  otherwise. Full image credits must be kept clear, visible, and unaltered.
